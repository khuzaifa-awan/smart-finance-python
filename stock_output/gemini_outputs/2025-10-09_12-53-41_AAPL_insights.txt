#### 2. Risks and Opportunities

**Opportunities:**

**Risks:**

*   **Increased Competition & Regulatory Scrutiny:** Intense competition in the tech sector, particularly in AI, and growing regulatory pressures globally (antitrust, app store fees) could impact Apple's business model and profitability.
*   **Reliance on iPhone Sales:** While services are growing, the iPhone still represents a substantial portion of revenue. Any slowdown in upgrades or sales could negatively impact overall performance.
*   **Supply Chain & Geopolitical Risks:** Apple's vast global supply chain is susceptible to disruptions from geopolitical tensions, trade disputes, or natural disasters.
*   **Macroeconomic Headwinds:** A global economic slowdown or reduced consumer spending could impact demand for premium-priced Apple products.
